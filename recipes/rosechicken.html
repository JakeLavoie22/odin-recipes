This dish is rather similar to a Bolognaise, but with more cream and the chicken replaces the ground beef!

Ingredients

  • 500 grams of Chicken
  • 250 grams of Tagliatelles pasta
  • 1 jar of tomato sauce
  • paprika
  • onions
  • Salt and pepper
  • Grated cheese

How to make it!

  1. Heat pan and add olive oil
  2. Add chopped onions, cook until brown
  3. Add chicken, cook until brown
  4. Add tomato sauce, cook for 25 mins
  5. Cook pasta
