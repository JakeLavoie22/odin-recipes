Protein Waffles

One of the best protein packed snacks out there.

Ingredients

  • 500 grams of flour
  • 250 grams of oat flower
  • 100 grams of protein powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 liter of milk
  • baking powder

How to make it!

  1. Mix all ingredients together
  2. let sit for 10 minutes
  3. butter the waffle machine and wait until hot
  4. poor waffle mix
  5. Cook for 5 minutes
