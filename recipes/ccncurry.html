Coconut Chicken Curry

Other than being healthy, Coconut Chicken curry is one of the tastiest meals on this list

Ingredients

  • 500 grams of Chicken
  • 500 grams of rice
  • 1 can of coconut milk
  • curry powder
  • Tomato paste
  • Salt and pepper
  • chicken stock

How to make it!

  1. Heat pan and add olive oil
  2. Add chopped onions, cook until brown
  3. Add chicken, cook until brown
  4. Add curry, cook for 5 mins
  5. Add tomato paste, broth, salt, and pepper
  6. Cook rice
